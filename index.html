About Me

I am a detail oriented Front-End developer with a passion for creating, repairing, building, and innovating. My diverse background in corporate and investing allows me to excel at logical thinking and creative problem solving, allowing development to be the perfect field for my skillset. I love dry, responsive, accesible code and bring expierence developing applications in Javascript, React, jQuery, CSS/SCSS, and HTML
